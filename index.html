Data management
Wipe delete log
Clears the list of tweets you've deleted. Previously deleted tweets will be treated as new on next import. Does not affect your library.
Clear all likes
Deletes your entire library — all tweets and images. Does not affect the delete log, so previously deleted tweets stay deleted.
Full reset
Wipes everything — library, images, delete log, and boards. The app returns to factory fresh state. Use before importing a new CSV to avoid duplicate conflicts.
